About Us

Welcome to HelpMitra, a student-driven platform designed to help students find solutions to their issues and explore opportunities across various fields.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a supportive environment where students can collaborate, share knowledge, and access career opportunities.

Our Vision

We aim to become the go-to platform for student networking, mentorship, and growth.

What We Offer

  • Discussion forums for students to ask and answer questions.
  • Career & internship opportunities to help students grow.
  • Study materials & resources for academic success.
  • Mentorship & networking with seniors and professionals.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate students and professionals working together to build a platform for student success.
